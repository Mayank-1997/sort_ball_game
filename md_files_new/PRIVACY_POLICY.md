# Privacy Policy for Ball Sort Puzzle

**Last Updated:** November 5, 2025

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Game Progress Data:** Level completion, scores, and achievements (stored locally and via Google Play Games Services when signed in)
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymous gameplay statistics to improve app performance
- **Advertising Data:** Advertising identifier for personalized ads through Google AdMob

### Voluntarily Provided Information
- **Google Play Games Profile:** When you choose to sign in to Google Play Games Services, we access your public profile information (display name and player ID) for leaderboards and achievements

## Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal contact information (email, phone number, address)
- Location data or GPS coordinates
- Camera, microphone, or photo access
- Contact lists or call logs
- Financial or payment information
- Health or fitness data
- Personal files or documents

## How We Use Information

### Game Functionality
- Save and sync your game progress across devices
- Display your scores on leaderboards
- Track achievements and milestones
- Provide cloud save functionality

### App Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve game design
- Monitor app performance and fix bugs
- Optimize loading times and gameplay experience

### Advertising
- Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
- Measure ad effectiveness and user engagement
- Provide option to remove ads through premium purchase

## Information Sharing

### Third-Party Services
We work with the following trusted partners:

**Google Play Games Services**
- Purpose: Leaderboards, achievements, and cloud save
- Data Shared: Game progress, scores, public profile information
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Google AdMob**
- Purpose: Advertisement display and analytics
- Data Shared: Advertising identifier, device information, app usage
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### No Sale of Personal Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their commercial purposes.

## Data Security

### Protection Measures
- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- Game progress is stored securely through Google Play Games Services
- We follow Google Play's security guidelines and best practices
- Regular security updates and monitoring

### Data Retention
- Game progress data is retained as long as you use the app
- Analytics data is retained for up to 2 years
- You can delete your data by uninstalling the app and clearing Google Play Games data

## Children's Privacy

### COPPA Compliance
- This app is safe for children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- If we become aware of any personal information collected from children under 13, we will delete it immediately
- Parents can contact us to review, delete, or stop further collection of their child's information

### Age-Appropriate Content
- Game content is suitable for all ages (rated Everyone/PEGI 3+)
- No chat features or communication with strangers
- No user-generated content or social sharing
- Family-friendly advertisements only

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control
- **Access:** View your game progress and statistics within the app
- **Deletion:** Uninstall the app to remove local data; sign out of Google Play Games to remove cloud data
- **Opt-out:** Disable personalized ads in your device settings

### Google Play Games
- Sign in is completely optional - you can play the full game without an account
- You can sign out at any time from the settings menu
- Signing out will disable leaderboards and cloud save features

### Advertising
- Reset your advertising ID in device settings to limit ad personalization
- Purchase the premium version to remove all advertisements
- Ads are provided by Google AdMob and follow Google's advertising policies
