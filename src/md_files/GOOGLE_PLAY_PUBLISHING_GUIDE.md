## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

### **Technical Mistakes**
- Publishing without proper testing on various devices
- Ignoring crash reports and ANRs
- Poor performance optimization
- Requesting unnecessary permissions

### **ASO Mistakes**
- Keyword stuffing in title/description
- Generic or unclear app icon
- Poor quality screenshots
- Not responding to user reviews

### **Marketing Mistakes**
- No pre-launch buzz building
- Ignoring user feedback post-launch
- Not tracking and analyzing metrics
- Focusing only on downloads, not retention
